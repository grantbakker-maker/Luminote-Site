Luminote Privacy Policy

Effective: October 21, 2025

Information We Collect

  • Account ID (if you sign in) to identify your account.
  • Journal content (stored on your device; optional sync if enabled).
  • Reminder/time‑capsule metadata to deliver notifications.
  • Anonymous analytics/crash logs (no journal content).

How We Use It

To save your notes, deliver reminders and “Future You” notifications, and improve stability and performance.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We use limited processors for sync, analytics, and notifications only.

Retention & Deletion

Your notes remain on your device unless you enable sync or backup. You can delete all app data at any time in Settings. If you create an account and wish to delete it, email support@luminoteapp.com.

Children

Luminote is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit and securely stored where supported by the platform.

Contact

Email: support@luminoteapp.com
